Cupuaçu

Native to the Brazilian Amazon, Cupuaçu is a fruit rich in flavonoids and vitamins A, B and C.
The butter extracted from this fruit is an excellent component in cosmetic formulation, as it helps maintain skin vitality and neutralizes the action of free radicals, thus protecting against its premature aging.

Brazil Nuts

Also originating in the Amazon, it is from this nut that a precious oil, highly nutritious, which contains fatty acids, phytosteroids and vitamins A and E. It is a rich source of magnesium, thiamine and has the highest concentrations of selenium, with antioxidant properties.

Copaiba

Copaiba is a native fruit of the Amazon, whose extracted oil has bactericidal function,
anti-inflammatory, antibiotic, and healing.

Andiroba

Dense canopy tree, reaching 30 meters in height, Andiroba has an oil extracted from the seeds, known for its use against sore throats and as an insect repellent, in addition to other medicinal properties. Its bark also has healing effects.

Muru Muru

Muru Muru is a palm seed, rich in fatty acids, mainly lauric acid, myristic acid, arginine and vitamins D and E. Muru Muru is used in a variety of ways as food, for the manufacture of butter, handicrafts and for palm heart extraction.
